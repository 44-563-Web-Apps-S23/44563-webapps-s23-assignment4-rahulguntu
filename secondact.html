MRS.DELANE.

I should be making myself ready, too. I must be in good time to see you being made an example of, Mr. Halvey. It is I, myself, was the first to say it; you will be a credit to the town. [_Goes._

HYACINTH.

[_In a tone of agony._]

I wish I had never seen Cloon.

MR.QUIRKE.

I'll come, to be sure. I forget what's this the meeting is about.
